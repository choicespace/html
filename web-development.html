Our Process For Web
Development Projects

 

We follow a strict SDLC process to get an idea to life with our in-house team and some standard project management tools such as Trello, Github, and Jira with a complete agile process to get the desired result. Our project management follows a step by step procedure to get successful delivery and get great return on investment.

Wireframe & Design

We meticulously craft wireframes that outline the blueprint of your project, ensuring optimal user experience and functionality. Our design process seamlessly merges creativity and functionality, creating visually captivating interfaces that resonate with your audience. Elevate your digital presence with our Wireframe & Design services, where every pixel is purposefully placed to bring your ideas to life

Development

Actual system development starts with the frontend/backend development wherein we put the soul into the design to make the design alive. The frontend helps the users to check the system from their end and play with it. Backend is the brain of the system which works closely with the frontend to make the system functioning.

Usability Testing

Usability testing is a crucial phase in the web development process that focuses on evaluating the user-friendliness and effectiveness of a website, application, or digital product. The primary goal of usability testing is to ensure that the end-users can interact with the system intuitively and efficiently, leading to a positive user experience.

Deployment

It’s the most important phase for SDLC that is more focused on robust delivery of the project on client server after the successful UAT, Delivery phase is most likely server side and defines the server configuration and maintenance of the system.

Maintenance & Support

After successful delivery of the project, it requires continuous monitoring and maintenance to make sure the project is functioning as expected. We provide 24*7 support with our dedicated team who helps our clients to maintain their projects and make return on investment without any hassle.
